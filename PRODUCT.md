# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

People in Juiz de Fora and nearby who need upholstery repair or restoration for sofas, chairs, armchairs, and similar furniture. They are comparing options, judging trust quickly, and want a local service that feels reliable, professional, and easy to contact.

## Product Purpose

RenovaJF presents the upholstery service clearly, builds trust fast, and turns interest into contact. The site should make it easy for visitors to understand the service, see proof of work, and start a conversation by WhatsApp, phone, or email.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, careful, professional. The tone should feel local and experienced, not generic or promotional.

Three-word personality: **reliable, crafted, direct**.

## Anti-references

- Generic agency or template landing pages with interchangeable hero sections.
- Overdesigned fintech or SaaS visuals that make the service feel impersonal.
- Loud neon gradients, glassmorphism, or AI-tool marketing aesthetics.
- Empty hype copy that talks around the service instead of showing it.
- Interfaces that hide the contact path or make it hard to ask for a quote.

## Design Principles

1. Make the service obvious within seconds.
2. Put trust signals, portfolio imagery, and contact actions where people expect them.
3. Keep the page visually calm enough that the workmanship can carry the brand.
4. Favor clear hierarchy and strong readability over decoration.
5. Optimize for mobile first, because quick contact is the main conversion path.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Keep contrast high, interactions keyboard-friendly, focus states visible, and motion restrained. The page must remain readable and usable on small screens and in low-attention browsing situations.
